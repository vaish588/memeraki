About MeMeraki

MeMeraki is a DPIIT-National Startup Award-winning venture that serves as a heartfelt home for India's traditional artists. We enable traditional artisans from some of the remotest corners of India to become digital creators for the first time. Through meaningful storytelling and a global online store, we help them share their craft with the world and earn a fair, dignified living. Trusted by 16,000+ patrons worldwide, MeMeraki offers the largest online collection of traditional Indian artworks, featuring 10,000+ products by 500+ artists across 300+ art forms, available globally.

Our mission is simple: to preserve India's heritage, celebrate our master artisans, and help them earn a fair, dignified living by connecting them directly with people who truly value their craft.
