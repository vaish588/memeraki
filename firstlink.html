The Architecture of Tradition in Modern Living

At MeMeraki, we believe that home decor is more than just aesthetics; it is a dialogue between the past and the present. Our collections bring the intricate details of Pichwai art from Rajasthan, the geometric precision of Madhubani from Bihar, and the vibrant tribal stories of Gond art directly into urban homes.

Our lookbook serves as a geographical map of Indian heritage preserved in contemporary spaces. From sprawling bungalows in Delhi to compact minimalist apartments in Mumbai and Bangalore, the MeMeraki touch adds a soul to every wall.
